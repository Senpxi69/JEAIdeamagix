About Essajees Atelier

With more than 350,000 sq. ft. spaces currently under construction across 12 cities, and over 100 completed projects in the ultra luxury interiors segment, Essajees Atelier is a rapidly expanding brand that has become synonymous with bespoke luxury interior projects.

Headed by Sarah Sham, and founded as an extension of her family’s 130 year old antiques dealership, Essajees Atelier is Sarah’s endeavour to bring her family’s legacy into the 21st century. With Jea, Essajees Atelier brings its razor-sharp attention to detail and second-to-none understanding of the interiors segment to luxury spaces in the Middle East region.

About Abra

Abra is a 30+ year old family business, headquartered in Dubai, delivering luxury brand spaces from Dubai to the world. They operate the region's largest purpose-built facility that caters to bespoke joinery and quality shopfitting in luxury retail.

Spanning approximately 200,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing space, and over 30+ years of industry experience, Abra is a well-established brand, synonymous with delivering the highest quality, bespoke spaces across the globe from their facilities in UAE and Saudi Arabia. With a 350+ strong team and comprehensive in-house capabilities, they have delivered stunning projects for leading retail locations across the world. Abra is also a partner of choice for top luxury conglomerates such as COTY, PUIG, L'oreal and LVMH.

At Jea, Abra brings their technical expertise and on-ground experience, with the promise of superior quality and professionalism in every project they execute.
